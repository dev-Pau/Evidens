Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information in Evidens (the Service, the App), and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

What types of data do we process?

In the context of the features andd services offered by Evidens, and subject to the purposes outlined in this Policy, Evidens may process the following categories of personal data, illustratively but not limited to:

  • Your identifying information: Name and surname, ID card (DNI), discipline, speciality.

  • Your contact information: Email address.

  • Data related to your device: Operating system, software version, unique device identifier.

For what purpose do we process your data?

In Evidens, we process the personal data that you provide to us, as well as those that derive from your relationship with us, for the following purposes outlined below:

  • Your identifying information: We use your name, surname and email address to provide you with secure access to Evidens. Additionally, we use this information to communicate with you when necessary, such as to properly address and attend your suggestions or when we update our policies.

  • Your healthcare identification information: We use your ID card or any other verification method to confirm your healthcare status, thereby granting you access to Evidens.

  • Data related to your device: We monitor the operating system and software version of your device to gain insights into the platforms utilized by our users. This information allows us to optimize our software for enhanced compatibility with your system. Furthermore, the utilization of your device's unique identifier serves as an additional security layer for your account, ensuring continuous access and mitigating the risk of data leaks.

For how long will we retain your data?

We retain your data for the duration necessary to deliver our Service. Data that becomes irrelevant due to updates is promptly removed from our database. Upon account deletion, all associated data, including your email address, personal details, unshared moments, and media within exclusive moments, is permanently erased.

Upon account deactivation, we maintain an archive for 30 days. Subsequent to this period, all information is irrevocably deleted, ensuring it cannot be recovered.

To which recipients will the data be communicated?

Evidens, in general, will not disclose your personal data to third parties except to comply with a legal obligation.

Likewise, in order to provide you with the described services, we require the support of service providers. These third parties may have access to your data and will act on our behalf, following our instructions. In no case will they have access to your data for their own purposes.

Is your data subject to transfer outside the European Economic Area?

In general, the processing of your data will take place within the European Economic Area.

What are your rights as the data subject?

Below are your rights as the data subject:

  • Access: You can confirm whether Evidens processes your personal data and inquire about the personal data included in Evidens' files..

  • Rectification: You can modify your personal data if it is inaccurate and complete any incomplete information.

  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data when, among other reasons, the data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

  • Objection: You can request that your personal data not be processed. Evidens will stop processing the data, except for compelling legitimate reasons, or the exercise or defense of possible claims.

  • Limitation: You can request the limitation of the processing of your data in the following cases:

    • While the challenge to the accuracy of your data is being verified.
    • When the processing is unlawful, and you oppose the deletion of your data, requesting the limitation of its use.
    • When Evidens no longer needs to process your data, but you need it for the exercise or defense of claims.
    • When you have objected to the processing of your data for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or for the satisfaction of a legitimate interest, while verifying whether the legitimate grounds for processing prevail over yours.

  • Portability: You can receive, in electronic format, the personal data you have provided to us and those obtained from your contractual relationship with Evidens, as well as transmit them to another entity.

    • The exercise of these rights is free, except in the case of manifestly unfounded or excessive requests. In the event of reasonable doubts about your identity, additional information may be requested to confirm it, allowing Evidens to respond to your request.
